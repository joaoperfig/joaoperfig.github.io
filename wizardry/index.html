Wizardry and Dwarven Shenanigans!

Join these Dwarves in Fumbling around to play Soccer with a Ball that's about as big as they are.

Download latest Alpha

Mastery of the Ball!

A fun and fast couch multiplayer game that'll have you and your friends on the edge of your seat. Each Wizard has a moba-style set of unique abilities that make each play new and unexpected. This game is being developed by a small team of Portuguese students.

Read More »

Unique Wizards

Wizards are designed with a theme/class in mind. Each Wizard features his own set of four unique and thematic abilities.

Local Multiplayer

The game was designed with local multiplayer in mind and supports any combination of keyboard and gamepad controlls.

Fun and Fast Gameplay

Abilities are fast to cast and have low cooldows that keet the ball rushing from one side of the field to the other. Try to keep up!

Explore available Wizards

Five Wizards have been designed and fully implemented to the game. About a dozen more are planned. Check them out!

Wizards

The Field

A simple soccer field with beveled corners to ensure the ball is always going somewhere important.

Planned Maps

Maps with different layouts/quirks are being planned and designed.

GameModes

GameModes include: pick Wizards, random Wizards, and random Abilities. Currently all games are first to 5 goals.
