About

This is a Fun-packed, Fast-paced game being developed by a small team of students.


Features

Unique Wizards

Wizards are designed with a theme/class in mind. Each Wizard features his own set of four unique and thematic abilities.
Aditionally, other Game Modes allow mashing up abilities into new unique combinations.





Local Multiplayer

The game was designed with local multiplayer in mind and supports any combination of keyboard and gamepad controlls.
In extreme scenarios, four players can be playing on one keyboard! (We recomment that you plug a second keyboard for this, it gets a little crowded)



Fun and Fast Gameplay

Abilities are fast to cast and have low cooldows that keet the ball rushing from one side of the field to the other. Try to keep up!
