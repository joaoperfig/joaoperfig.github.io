Wizards

The following is the list of wizards Currently implemented into the game.



Pyromancer

Fire themed wizard.
Stronger at attacking.
Abilities:
   Normal Kick.
   Temporarily doubled speed.
   Small fireball that homes to the ball.
   Big non-homing fireball.
Thinks he's hot.



RuneScribe

Rune themed Wizard
Support and Defense Wizard. Places stationary Runes on the Field.
Abilities:
   Normal Kick.
   Places Rune that gives speed buffs and stuns.
   Places Defense Rune that explodes.
   Places Rune he can teleport to.
Fingerpainting enthusiast.



Thaumaturge

Force themed Wizard
Can defend from attacking positions.
Abilities:
   Normal Kick.
   Dashes forward.
   Pulls the ball to himself.
   Launches a slow moving sphere that pulls the ball to itself.
Can't pronnounce his own name.



Occultist

Shadow themed Wizard
Moves quickly across the field, confusing the enemy.
Abilities:
   Normal Kick.
   Teleports forward.
   Swaps positions with closest enemy.
   Teleports to ball, pushes ball away on second activation.
His mom thinks its a phase.



Warlock

Tentacle themed Wizard
Great ontrol of the game.
Abilities:
   Normal Kick.
   Dashes forward, leaving a trail that slows enemies.
   Spawns a Tentacle that catches and throws the ball in the desired direction.
   Makes the ball unreachable to enemies.
Into Tentacle Hentai stuff.



Necromancer

Death themed Wizard
Really annoying.
Abilities:
   Normal Kick.
   Teleports forward leaving corpse behind, returns to corpse and after 3 seconds.
   Spawns a Zombie that runs to other players and messes their stuff.
   Spawns a Skeleton that runs to the ball and pushes it.
"This is to die for" and similar puns.
